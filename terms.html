Lexso SMS Terms and Conditions

Lexso provides SMS notifications for internal system testing, operational alerts, and platform notifications.

Program Description

Users who opt into the Lexso SMS program may receive low-volume informational text messages related to system testing, operational alerts, and platform notifications.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies depending on testing activity and system events.

Opt-In

Users opt in by providing their mobile phone number to the Lexso team and requesting participation in the SMS testing program.

Opt-Out

Users may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any SMS message.

Help

Users may reply HELP for assistance.

Fees

Message and data rates may apply depending on the user's mobile carrier plan.
